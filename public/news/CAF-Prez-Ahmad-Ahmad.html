CAF PREZ AHMAD AHMAD PRONOUNCE :They Are Our Hope And Future.

April 5th, 2018

Confederation of African football President endorses the New Online TV channel in his visit to Accra for the 2017 Aiteo CAF awards gala.The T.V channel He believes will be a major boost to African football. The President of Malagasy football federation as well as CAF Chief And his 1st Vice President Kwesi Nyantakyi endorsed the New Online TV channel after the CEO of "Kingstv",enterprising Sports broadcaster King Halid disclosed the mission and vision of the Online TV channel to them.

The CAF President heartfelt request to " Kingstv" in the presence of entourage of CAF executive's including his 1st Vice Kwesi Nyantakyi double as Ghana F.A Chief are to use the channel to inform, educate and promote Ghana and African football as a whole .He stated that ,Television is currently one of the most admired mass media channels in the world and its growth is seen all over the world everyday because of its conveyance of speech and moving pictures.

Mr Ahmad Ahmad who dethroned the longest serving CAF president Issa Hayatou won his election In March 2017, he was voted in as President of CAF, winning 34 votes to Issa Hayatou's 20. He becomes the 7th President of the body. Before then, he was a member of the Caf Executive Committee.
